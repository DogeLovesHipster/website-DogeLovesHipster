About your site

Badger Creek at Rugg Road Near Gresham, OR

The USGS monitoring site "Badger Creek at Rugg Road Near Gresham, OR" (site number 452737122231400) is located in Clackamas County, Oregon. This site monitors a stream within the hydrologic unit 17090012 and has a drainage area of 2.29 square miles. The USGS Oregon Water Science Center maintains records for this site, with water data available online dating back to 2007. The data collected include measurements of turbidity and various water quality parameters, which can be accessed and visualized through the USGS National Water Dashboard.
